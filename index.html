Welcome to the Test4Theory Leaderboard!

This leaderboard and Billionaires Club list the users who are contributing the most simulations and jobs in Test4Theory.

The leaderboard sorts user's stats according to:

  • Number of events
  • Number of processed jobs, and
  • Nominal CPU time (in months)

The charts only include the top contributors, so you may not find yourself in it.

In order to check these values for your account, check your Profile page in the Test4Theory project.

Billionaires Club: This "club" is a time line showing those users that have contributed more than 1 Billion simulated events! over the time.

Info The Leaderboard & Billionaires Club are in beta mode and some users and/or hosts are not listed properly due to some bugs that we are investigating (minimum BOINC version >= 6.12).
